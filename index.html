Beyonce CD Cover

Multi-Program Project Design

For this project, Adobe Indesign, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop were used to create this CD Booklet. This was a unique project because it combined everything we had learned over the course of the semester into one project. This ranged from learning how to alter images in Photoshop to creating our own vectors in Illustrator to making the layout through the use of InDesign. One of the major aspects of this project was the post design which was printing. By this stage of the project, we were expected to know and understand the technical side of printing in order to create a front and back CD booklet. This in itself was prepatory to understanding many future projects as it taught us lessons that will be forever important.
